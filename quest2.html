Terreling Forest

The road is perilous, and you are jumped by bandits. But you have a quick wit and a sharp tongue – you offer the bandits some of the reward if they help you slay the dragon. They accept and you have now gathered a small party to help you on your quest

You find your path through the forest longer and more tiresome than you expected – with the bandits sometimes slowing you down - with just enough supplies to get you to the dragon's liar, you fear they'll run out before you get there.

Do you keep your friends or decide to brave the last part of your journey on your own?
